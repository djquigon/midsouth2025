'The Digital Suite' refers to the services
that are essential for the M&R Digital Dept's role, i.e. Google Analytics, Google Ads, CallRail, etc.

Set these services up, in this order:

1. [[the-digital-suite.analytics]]
2. [[the-digital-suite.tag-manager]]
3. [[the-digital-suite.google-ads]]
4. [[the-digital-suite.callrail]]
5. [[the-digital-suite.search-console]] & [[the-digital-suite.bing-webmaster-tools]]
    1. (setup of _these 2_ take place during the Post-Live phase)

### Google Service Linking / Association

To maximize performance across all Google Services, they must be linked to / associated with each other.

-   [Connect Google Ads to Google Analytics](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9379420?hl=en#zippy=%2Cin-this-article)
-   [Connect Search Console to Google Analytics](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/10737381?hl=en)
-   [Connect Search Console to Google Ads](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7529561?hl=en)
